🧘 Conversational AI Coach

A multilingual voice-enabled conversational AI coaching assistant designed to simulate reflective, ICF-style coaching conversations using speech recognition, LLM prompting, and AI voice interaction.

View GitHub Watch Demo

Why This Project

This project was built to explore whether conversational AI can support reflective coaching interactions rather than simply provide answers or advice.


The focus was on creating a more natural conversational experience through multilingual voice interaction, conversational continuity, and coaching-oriented prompting aligned with reflective questioning practices.

Core Features

🎙️ Voice Interaction

Users can speak naturally through microphone input and receive AI-generated spoken coaching responses.

🌍 Multilingual Support

Supports English, Hindi, and Kannada conversational coaching flows.

🧠 Conversational Memory

Maintains session continuity across multiple coaching turns for more natural interactions.

🧭 Reflective Coaching Prompts

Designed to avoid direct advice and instead encourage reflection through open-ended questioning.

Technical Design Decisions

Why Gradio?

Chosen for rapid AI prototyping, native audio components, and simplified Hugging Face deployment workflows.

Why Whisper?

Used for multilingual speech recognition and reliable transcription quality across conversational input.

Why Prompt Constraints?

Coaching prompts were intentionally constrained to avoid advice-giving and maintain reflective dialogue patterns.

Why Voice-First?

Voice interaction creates a more natural coaching experience compared to traditional text-only AI chat interfaces.

Privacy & Session Safety

  • Sessions are designed as transient conversational interactions.
  • No intentional long-term storage of coaching conversations was implemented.
  • API keys remain server-side protected.
  • The prototype is intended for conversational AI experimentation purposes.
  • The assistant is designed for reflective coaching interaction and not therapy or medical advice.
